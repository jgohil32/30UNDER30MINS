About Us

We are two university students who understand the struggles of time when it comes to cooking.

In our busy lives, sadly, it is easy to forget about looking after yourself!

So we designed 30 Under 30 Mins to help people like us find healthy recipes to cook within 30 minutes.

We update a recipe a day and even look out for ingredients we have listed before to minimise food waste!

Join us and try a healthy 30 Under 30 Mins month with us!
